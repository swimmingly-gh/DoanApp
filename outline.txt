The Doan Ryo: the ceremony work group

The Doan ryo are the orchestra of the meditation hall in the practice roles of tenken, doan, and
kokyo (see below). Training is conducted by the Ino (head of meditation hall).

 Tenken – Bells and clackers for zazen and kinhin, and during the service plays the wooden mokugyo drum.
 Doan – Rings the bells during the service.
 Kokyo – Leads the chanting during the service.
